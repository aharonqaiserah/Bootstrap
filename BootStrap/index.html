Everday Healthy Eating

Healthy eating is a dietary approach that prioritizes nutrient-rich foods while limiting processed foods, added sugars, and unhealthy fats

Prioritize Fruits and Vegetables:

  1. Eat at least 5 portions of a variety of fruits and vegetables daily
  2. Include dark green vegetables like broccoli, kale, and spinach.
  3. Choose berries and other fruits for their antioxidant benefits

Choose Whole Grains:

  1. Base meals on higher-fiber starchy foods like whole wheat bread, rice, and pasta
  2. Opt for whole grains over refined grains for added fiber and nutrients
